About The Project

A Secure, Robust Architecture

MyFeeStatus is built on the Django framework, combining powerful ORM models with dynamic front-end micro-interactions. The application structures students, academic structures, and transactions cleanly, ensuring high reliability.

  • Extensible Models: Customize tuition, exam, and lab structures per course or semester.
  • Robust Validations: Prevent payment mismatches or double ledger postings automatically.
  • Secure Sessions: Django's built-in session storage and authentication safeguards account privacy.
